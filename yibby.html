Inside Yibby.ai

Emotion-First Discovery

The Yibby homepage introduces a revolutionary approach to gift shopping. Instead of browsing endless product categories, you start with who you're shopping for and how you want them to feel.

  • Choose between "For Her" and "For Him" pathways
  • Clean, inviting design with immediate call-to-action
  • Smart recommendations powered by human-curated data
  • Connected to trusted merchants like Amazon, Etsy, and TikTok Shop

Curated Gift Collections

The gift browsing experience is organized by categories that matter - Health & Wellness, Hobbies & Activities, and Home & Kitchen. Each collection is thoughtfully curated based on real gifting data.

  • Shop by personality, interest, occasion, and price
  • Real customer stories and verified 5-star testimonials
  • Smart filtering to narrow down the perfect gift quickly
  • Price-conscious options including "Under $50" collections

Personalized Pathways

Whether shopping for him or her, Yibby adapts its recommendations based on the recipient's persona. The platform remembers your preferences and learns from every interaction.

  • Tailored categories for different recipient types
  • Occasion-based filtering (Birthday, Anniversary, Christmas, Valentine's Day)
  • Cookie-based preference persistence for return visits
  • No account required to start discovering gifts

Browse by Feeling

Yibby's search experience goes beyond keywords. Explore trending gift types, browse by emotional categories, and discover gifts that match the feeling you want to express.

  • Trending searches: Spa gifts, Tech gadgets, Luxury items
  • Gift types: Unique, Classic, Luxury, Practical, Personalized, Experience
  • Full-text search across the entire curated catalog
  • Real-time results with dynamic filtering
