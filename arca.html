Platform Overview

Inside Arca.ph

AI-Forward Careers

Arca's landing page immediately communicates its mission: empowering Filipino professionals with AI skills and connecting them to global opportunities. The platform combines training, community, and job placement.

  • Dual pathways: "Find Jobs" for talent, "Find Talent" for employers
  • Free skill tracks in product, design, research, and automation
  • 72+ registered professionals and growing
  • Direct connection between US employers and Filipino talent

Remote Job Listings

The jobs board features curated remote positions from verified employers. Each listing is designed for Filipino professionals with AI-augmented skill sets, ensuring relevance and quality.

  • Full-time remote positions from verified companies
  • Roles spanning Product Management, Marketing, Operations, and AI Research
  • All candidates are pre-vetted through Arca's training programs
  • Direct payment to talent with no markups or commissions

Simple, Transparent Pricing

Arca uses a straightforward pay-per-job model with no subscriptions or hidden fees. Employers can post jobs or use the premium Arca Scout concierge service for hands-off hiring.

  • Standard Job Posting: $299 with no expiration date
  • 3 free edits within 24 hours of posting
  • Arca Scout (Direct Hire): $4,000 concierge service
  • Qualified profiles delivered within 48 hours

Arca Scout: Concierge Hiring

For employers who want a hands-off hiring experience, Arca Scout provides a dedicated hiring specialist who handles the entire recruitment process from search to placement.

  • Trial-verified talent pre-vetted through real project work
  • Dedicated hiring specialist assigned to each engagement
  • Fast matching with 48-hour turnaround time
  • Free consultation with no commitment required
